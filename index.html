The NamesWe Carry

A Memorial to the 2,977

Scroll

A curved wall of glass carries the names of all who were lost. Within its surface, every visitor sees their own reflection among them — the living and the remembered, together. A place to pause, to remember, and to carry them forward.

The Concept

The Reflection

The wall is glass, not stone. When you stand before it, the names of the dead and your own reflection share the same surface. We do not only look at them — we stand among them.
